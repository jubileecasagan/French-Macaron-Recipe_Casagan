French Macaron Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Egg Whites
  • Cream of Tartar
  • Superfine Sugar
  • Fine Almond Flour
  • Gel Food Coloring (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add cream of tartar to your aged room temperature egg whites and beat until very soft peaks form. This takes a few minutes of beating.
  2. Now add your superfine sugar in 3 additions and beat until stiff glossy peaks form
  3. Then add your optional gel food coloring. It's important to add gel food coloring to the egg whites directly, instead of the finished macaron batter.
  4. Set your stiff egg whites aside.
  5. Sift the almond flour and confectioners' sugar together in a large bowl.
  6. In 3 separate additions, begin slowly folding in the beaten egg whites in a circular motion. I always eyeball the amount.
  7. After only 1 addition, the batter will be very thick and dry.
  8. The batter will begin to loosen up after you add the 2nd and 3rd addition.
  9. After that 3rd addition of egg whites, you're at the macaronage stage. You need to keep folding and deflating air until you reach the perfect consistency.
